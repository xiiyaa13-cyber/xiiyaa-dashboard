Daily Briefing

February 2, 2026

Big Picture

Major indexes finished sharply higher Monday to open February. The Dow added 515 points (1.1%), the S&P 500 rose 0.5% and just missed a new closing record, and the Nasdaq gained 0.6%. Memory stocks led gains—SanDisk surged 15%, Western Digital jumped 8%—while Caterpillar and Walmart paced the Dow with gains of 5% and 4%. Trump announced a trade deal with India cutting reciprocal tariffs from 25% to 18% in exchange for India halting Russian oil purchases, and nominated Kevin Warsh to succeed Powell as Fed Chair. Nvidia fell nearly 3% on reports its $100B OpenAI investment had stalled.
